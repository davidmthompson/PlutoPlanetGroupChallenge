Pluto

  • Discovered in 1930
  • Takes 248 Earth years to orbit our Sun
  • Days on pluto are 153 Hours roughly 6.4 Earth Days
  • Pluto is 1,473 miles which is smaller than the USA
  • Pluto sometimes closer to the sun then neptune
  • 1930: Clyde Tombaugh discovers Pluto at the Lowell Observatory.
  • 1978: Scientists discover Charon, Pluto’s largest moon.
  • 2006: The IAU reclassifies Pluto as a "Dwarf Planet."
  • 2015: NASA’s New Horizons spacecraft completes its historic flyby.
  • 2019: Data reveals Pluto has "Ice Volcanoes" and a liquid ocean beneath its crust.
